Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mathy ("the App") handles your information. We take your privacy seriously and have designed the App to collect as little data as possible.

Mathy does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The App operates entirely on your device.

The only data stored is your local game progress (scores, level, settings), which is saved on your device using iOS system storage and never leaves it.

Mathy does not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs that collect personal data.

Children

The App does not knowingly collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13.
