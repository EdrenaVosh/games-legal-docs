Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Untangle ("the App") handles your information. We take your privacy seriously and have designed the App to collect as little data as possible.

Data We Collect

Untangle does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The App operates entirely on your device with no internet connection required.

The following data is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted:

  • Haptic feedback preference — on/off setting.
  • Game run history — number of levels completed and time elapsed per session. Used to display your personal history in-app.
  • Review prompt timestamp — the date the App Store rating prompt was last shown, to avoid showing it too frequently.

Sharing Your Results

The App includes an optional Share feature that generates an image of your result and opens the iOS system share sheet. This action is initiated entirely by you. If you choose to save the image to your photo library, iOS will request permission to access Photos. The App itself does not access, read, or store photos beyond this save action.

App Store Review

After completing a number of sessions, the App may prompt you to rate it using the standard iOS review dialog provided by Apple (SKStoreReviewController). No personal data is collected or transmitted by this prompt. Apple's own Privacy Policy governs how App Store ratings are handled on their end.

Third-Party Services

Untangle does not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs that collect personal data. All features operate locally on your device.

Children

The App does not knowingly collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted here with an updated effective date.
